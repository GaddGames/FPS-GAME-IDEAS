Game Ideas - Open World Adventure

Game Concept

Set in an expansive open world, players embark on a perilous adventure to defeat enemy strongholds. These strongholds are guarded by formidable foes and clearing them unlocks new weapons, gear, and attachments.

Death and Extraction Mechanics

If a player is shot without wearing any armor plates, they die instantly and lose all their items. However, if the player manages to extract in time, they can choose which items to carry into the next round, preserving their progress and gear.

Objective

To complete the game, players must clear all the strongholds scattered throughout the open world. Each stronghold presents unique challenges and requires strategic planning to overcome.
